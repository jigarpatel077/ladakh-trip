NATURE VIEW

Best Places in Ladakh

Sham Valley

Sham Valley, also known as Lower Ladakh, is a beautiful region located in the western part of Ladakh, in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir.

Nubra valley

Nubra Valley is a high-altitude cold desert located in the northernmost part of Ladakh, in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir.

Pangong

The lake is situated at an altitude of over 14,000 feet above sea level and is known for its stunning blue waters and picturesque surroundings.

Chang la-leh

Magnetic Hill is a natural wonder located on the Leh-Kargil highway. The hill has a magnetic force that can pull vehicles uphill, defying the laws of gravity.

REVIEW

What Tourist Says

Aimee Garcia

@aimeegarcia

Ladakh is a region located in the northernmost part of India, nestled between the Karakoram mountain range in the north and the Himalayas in the south. It is a high-altitude desert and is known for its stark and breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and adventurous outdoor activities..

Ella Lopez

@ellalopez

Ladakh was historically an independent kingdom, and its culture is influenced by Tibetan Buddhism. The region is home to many ancient monasteries, such as Hemis Monastery, Thiksey Monastery, and Shey Monastery.

Lauren German

@laurengermanl

Tourists often visit Ladakh to explore its stunning natural beauty, which includes high-altitude lakes such as Pangong Tso and Tso Moriri, and the Nubra Valley, which is famous for its sand dunes and Bactrian camels. Visitors can also go trekking.
